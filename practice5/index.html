About

Danil Architects a large innovative construction holding, a leader in the real estate market of Kazakhstan. The holding has been operating since 1995 and during this time has earned a reputation as a professional and reliable partner. The main goal of the company's activity is the confident consolidation of the position of Construction Tech No. 1 in Central Asia.

Danil Architects today is a diversified holding, the structure of which is made up of divisions and directorates in various areas of construction, development and engineering
